    About Us

    Sports Events House Sdn Bhd is a sports event registration, consultancy & management company specializing in organizing cycling and running events in Malaysia. Setup in June 2013, we have served more than 50 clients throughout our 4 years of operation, ranging from cycling and running clubs, property developers to multinational companies.Our management and technical team are one of the pioneers in industry with more than 20 years of experience in organizing sports events. A few of our flagship projects include Sunsuria Foodiethon 2016, To The Fore Movie Ride, Symphony Life That ‘70s Run, XBT 3 Hours Challenge, Kampar Majesty Ride 2017 and etc.

    Event Consultation

    Provide technical and logistics know-how to clients on organizing successful events

    Event Management

    Strategic research and route planning, safety management, gimmick & activities planning, event day execution.

    Event Registration

    Provide online and manual event registration & payment collection services to event organizers

    Event Marketing & Promotion

    E-mail & SMS marketing, social media advertising, Sports Magazine partnerships and etc.

    Event Apparel & Premium Items

    Event T-shirt, jerseys, finisher medals, insurance coverage, bib numbers, timing system, E-certificate and etc

    Other Services

    Event official website setup, event video and photo production, visual & graphic design and etc.
